🔫 About the Game

DualStrike is a fast-paced 2-player FPS where you and a friend face off in an intense arena. Designed for keyboard-sharing or network play, it’s all about quick reflexes, sharp aim, and strategy.

🕹️ Player 1 Controls

  • Move: W / A / S / D
  • Shoot: Space
  • Reload: R

🎮 Player 2 Controls

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: Enter
  • Reload: Shift
